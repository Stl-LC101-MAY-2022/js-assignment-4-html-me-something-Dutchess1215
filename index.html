A Few Simple Camp Songs and Skits

This is just a few songs and a skit I remember from my time spent working on a Boy Scout Summer Camp Staff

  • The Greatest Story I Never Heard
  • The Skunk Song
  • Purple Stew

The Greatest Story I Never Heard

Ladies and Gentleman! Dogs and cats! Cross-eyed mosquitos and bowlegged ants! Admission is free, so pay at the door, pull up a chair and sit on the floor. I come before you, to stand behind you to tell you a story I know nothing about!
One bright morning in the middle of the night, two dead boys got up to fight! Back to back they faced each other, drew their swords and shot each other!
Now the deaf policeman heard this noise and got up to shoot those two dead boys. and if you don't believe my story, I promise it's true, just ask the blind man! He saw it too.
After finishing always have to do it faster, after doing it faster tell em you can do it backwards at which point you obviously just turn around and do it all again.

Welllllll I didn't take it out and the little skunk said 'You better take it out or you'd had! Take it out! Take it out! Take it out! Take it Out! Remoooove it!'

Purple Stew

We're making a purple stew!
Whip it Whip it! Whip it good!
We're making a purple stew!
Scooby dooby doo!
With purple potatoes and purple tomatoes and we want you!
(point at someone, make them stand up and sing along. After a few times through change you to everybody and get everyone up and involved)
